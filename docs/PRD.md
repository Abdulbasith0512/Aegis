# AegisAI – Product Requirements Document

## Vision

Build the world's first AI Governance Operating System capable of supervising autonomous AI agents in banking environments while ensuring transparency, resilience, compliance, and trust.

---

## Problem Statement

Modern financial institutions increasingly rely on AI models for fraud detection, AML, KYC, credit scoring, and customer service.

Current AI systems suffer from:

- Limited explainability
- Lack of governance
- Poor resilience under failures
- Model drift
- Weak monitoring
- Vendor model risks
- Regulatory compliance challenges

AegisAI solves these problems.

---

## Objectives

- Govern AI agents
- Explain AI decisions
- Monitor AI health
- Detect model drift
- Stress-test AI continuously
- Simulate failures
- Manage human approvals
- Generate compliance reports
- Build customer trust
